<p><p>The Human Proximity Sensor market offers two main types of sensors: the Two-Wire Proximity Sensor and the DC Three-Wire Proximity Sensor. The Two-Wire Proximity Sensor is designed to detect human presence by emitting infrared light and measuring the reflection. It requires only two wires for power and signal transmission, making it simpler to install. On the other hand, the DC Three-Wire Proximity Sensor also detects human proximity using infrared technology but requires three wires for power, ground, and signal transmission. This sensor provides greater flexibility in terms of distance and responsiveness.</p></p>
