<p><p>Pharmacy Information Systems (PIS) are used to effectively manage information and processes within pharmacies. There are three main types of PIS markets: web-based, on-premises, and cloud-based. </p><p>Web-based PIS allows users to access the system via a web browser, providing flexibility and convenience for multiple users across different locations. On-premises PIS is installed locally on pharmacy servers, ensuring full control and customization but limiting accessibility. Cloud-based PIS operates on remote servers, offering scalability, cost-efficiency, and easy access from various devices. Each market type caters to different pharmacy needs and priorities in managing information and operations.</p></p>
