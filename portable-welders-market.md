<p><p>The portable welders market comprises three main types of machines: manual welding machines, semi-automatic welding machines, and automatic welding machines. Manual welding machines require operators to control and manipulate the welding process manually. Semi-automatic welding machines have both manual and automatic features, with operators controlling some aspects while the machine handles others. Automatic welding machines require minimal human intervention, as they are programmed to perform the entire welding process independently. These types cater to different user preferences and work requirements, offering varying levels of control and automation.</p></p>
