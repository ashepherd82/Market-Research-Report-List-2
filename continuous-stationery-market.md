<p><strong>The Continuous Stationery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the continuous stationery market, there are two main types: single layer and multi-layer. Single layer stationery consists of a single sheet of paper that is commonly used for general office purposes. It is suitable for printing invoices, receipts, and other simple documents. On the other hand, multi-layer stationery is composed of several interconnected sheets with carbon paper in between. This enables the creation of multiple copies of the same document simultaneously, making it ideal for invoices, purchase orders, and other documents that require duplicate copies.</p></p>
