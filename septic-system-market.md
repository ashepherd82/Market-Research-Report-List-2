<p><strong>The Septic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Gravity Septic System</li><li>Conventional-Pump Septic System</li><li>Pressure Manifold Septic System</li><li>Low-Pressure Pipe (LPP) Septic System</li><li>Drip Disposal Septic Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septic system market consists of various types. Conventional Gravity Septic Systems rely on the natural force of gravity to move wastewater from the house to the tank and the drainfield. Conventional-Pump Septic Systems use a pump to transport wastewater. Pressure Manifold Septic Systems evenly distribute wastewater using a series of pipes and valves. Low-Pressure Pipe (LPP) Septic Systems use a pump to distribute effluent through small diameter pipes. Drip Disposal Septic Systems release wastewater gradually through drip tubing. Other types include Aerobic Treatment Units, Mound Systems, and Sand Filters. These systems vary in design and technology to meet different site requirements.</p></p>
