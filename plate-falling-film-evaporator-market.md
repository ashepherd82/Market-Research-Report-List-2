<p><strong>The Plate Falling Film Evaporator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Effect</li><li>Multiple-Effect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plate falling film evaporators are widely used in industries for efficient separation of liquid and vapor phase mixtures. In the single-effect market, only one evaporator plate is present, leading to lower efficiency and higher energy consumption. Conversely, multiple-effect evaporators consist of multiple plates arranged in a series, enabling the reuse of heat energy from one plate to the next, resulting in higher efficiency and reduced energy consumption. This makes multiple-effect evaporators more cost-effective and preferred in various industries.</p></p>
