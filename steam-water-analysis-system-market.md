<p><ul><li>Condensate Analysis</li><li>Boiler Feed Water Analysis</li><li>Boiler Water Analysis</li><li>Saturated Steam Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam and water analysis system (SWAS) market consists of various types such as condensate analysis, boiler feed water analysis, boiler water analysis, and saturated steam analysis. Condensate analysis involves analyzing the quality of steam condensate to ensure purity and detect any contamination. Boiler feed water analysis focuses on monitoring the quality of water fed into the boiler system to prevent corrosion or scaling. Boiler water analysis involves testing the water within the boiler for various parameters such as pH, conductivity, and dissolved oxygen. Saturated steam analysis assesses the quality of steam leaving the boiler system to ensure it meets required standards.</p></p>
