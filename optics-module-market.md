<p><p>The optics module market is categorized into two main types: single mode optical modules and multi-mode optical modules. Single-mode modules are designed to transmit a single ray of light, which makes them ideal for long-distance communication systems. Multi-mode modules, on the other hand, are suitable for shorter distance communication as they allow multiple rays of light to be transmitted simultaneously. Both types serve different communication needs and are used in various industries for data transmission, networking, and telecommunications applications.</p></p>
