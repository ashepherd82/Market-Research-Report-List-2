Firstly, the increasing prevalence of foot-related disorders, especially among the elderly population, is driving the demand for these devices. Moreover, the rise in sports injuries and accidents that result in foot-related problems is further fueling the demand for foot care devices.</p><p>Advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative and efficient foot care devices.
