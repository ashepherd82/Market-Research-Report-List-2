<p><p>Water treatment chemicals, also known as additives, are used in the oil and gas industry to treat water before it is reused or discharged. Different types of chemicals are used for specific purposes. Corrosion inhibitors prevent metal equipment from corroding. Scale inhibitors prevent the formation of scales on the equipment. Coagulants and flocculants help remove solid particles from water. Biocides and disinfectants eliminate bacteria and pathogens. Chelating agents bind with metal ions to prevent their interference. Anti-foaming agents control foam formation. PH adjusters and stabilizers maintain water pH levels. Other chemicals are used for various purposes in the water treatment process.</p></p>
