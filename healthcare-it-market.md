<p><p>The healthcare IT market consists of two primary types of deployments: local deployment and cloud deployment. Local deployment refers to the installation and management of healthcare IT systems on-site, within the organization's infrastructure. This entails purchasing and maintaining hardware and software within the organization. In contrast, cloud deployment involves the use of remote servers to store and process healthcare data. Cloud deployment offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as organizations can access and manage their healthcare IT systems via the internet without the need for extensive infrastructure.</p></p>
