<p><p>Solid cored solder wire, also known as flux core solder wire, is a type of solder wire that contains a flux core inside. Flux is a chemical cleaning agent that removes oxidation and other impurities from the metal surfaces being joined together. The flux core in solid cored solder wire eliminates the need for a separate flux application, making it easier and more convenient to use.</p><p>The solid cored solder wire market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for electronic components and the growing trend of miniaturization in the electronics industry are the primary drivers for the market growth.
