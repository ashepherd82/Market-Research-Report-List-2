<p><p>Bolts are commonly available in two market types: Half Screw Bolts and Full Screw Bolts. Half Screw Bolts have threads only on one-half of their length, making them suitable for applications where quick assembly and disassembly are required. On the other hand, Full Screw Bolts have threads along their entire length, offering enhanced stability and resistance. Both market types cater to different needs; Half Screw Bolts for versatility, and Full Screw Bolts for strength and rigidity. Understanding the specific requirements of each application helps determine the appropriate bolt type for optimal performance.</p></p>
