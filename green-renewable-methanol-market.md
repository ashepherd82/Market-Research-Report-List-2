It can be used as a fuel, as a substitute for traditional methanol, or as a raw material for various chemical processes. On the other hand, E-methanol is produced by combining hydrogen obtained from renewable energy sources with carbon dioxide. E-methanol is considered a more sustainable alternative to traditional methanol as it does not rely on fossil fuels for production.</p></p>
