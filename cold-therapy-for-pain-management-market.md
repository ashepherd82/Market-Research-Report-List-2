<p><p>Cold therapy, also known as cryotherapy, is a pain management technique that involves applying cold temperatures to reduce pain and inflammation in the body. It is commonly used to treat acute injuries such as sprains, strains, and post-surgical pain. Cold therapy works by constricting blood vessels, reducing blood flow to the affected area, and numbing the nerve endings, thereby providing pain relief.</p><p>The global cold therapy for pain management market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
