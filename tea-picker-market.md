<p><p>There are two main types of tea picker markets: Selective Picking Machine and Non-Selective Picking Machine. In the Selective Picking Machine market, machines are designed to only pick the highest quality, fully matured tea leaves. They are programmed to differentiate and select the leaves based on their size, color, and maturity level. On the other hand, the Non-Selective Picking Machine market includes machines that pick all the tea leaves, regardless of their quality or maturity. These machines are usually cheaper and more commonly used in tea production.</p></p>
