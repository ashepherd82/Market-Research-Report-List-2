<p><p>The subsea umbilicals market consists of various types, including hydraulic umbilicals, electro-hydraulic umbilicals, and electro-fiber optical hydraulic umbilicals. Hydraulic umbilicals are used to transmit hydraulic fluids for various subsea operations. Electro-hydraulic umbilicals function by combining both hydraulic and electrical transmission capabilities. Electro-fiber optical hydraulic umbilicals, on the other hand, incorporate hydraulic, electrical, and fiber optic transmission for enhanced data and communication capabilities. Lastly, other types of umbilicals may include specialized configurations tailored to specific subsea project requirements. These umbilicals play a crucial role in supporting the operations of underwater equipment and devices.</p></p>
