<p><p>Dental implant abutments are prosthetic components used to connect dental implants to replacement teeth. Straight abutments are the most commonly used type and are used when the implant is placed in the ideal position and angulation. Angled abutments, on the other hand, are used when the implant is placed at an angle and require a customized abutment to ensure proper alignment of the replacement tooth. Both types play a crucial role in achieving a functional and esthetic outcome in dental implant restorations.</p></p>
