<p><p>Video pico projectors are compact devices used to project videos and images onto a larger screen. There are two main types of technology used in these projectors - DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to create the image, offering high contrast and bright colors. LCOS projectors use liquid crystals to create the image, offering superior image quality and better black levels. Both types have their unique advantages and are widely used in various applications, including professional presentations and home entertainment.</p></p>
