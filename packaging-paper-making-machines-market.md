<p><p>Packaging paper making machines are classified into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines operate independently, without the need for manual intervention, making them highly efficient and time-saving. These machines can perform various functions such as feeding raw materials, regulating the process, and packaging the final product automatically. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human involvement, primarily for loading raw materials and monitoring the process. While they offer some automation, they may require occasional manual adjustments. Both types serve the purpose of manufacturing packaging paper but differ in their level of automation.</p></p>
