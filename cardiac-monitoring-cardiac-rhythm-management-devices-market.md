<p><p>Cardiac monitoring and cardiac rhythm management devices are used to monitor and regulate the electrical activity and rate of the heart. The market is divided into various types of devices. ECG devices record the electrical signals of the heart to diagnose any abnormalities. Implantable loop recorders can monitor heart activity for extended periods, helping diagnose irregular heart rhythms. Cardiac output monitoring devices measure the volume of blood the heart pumps per minute. Defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythms, and pacemakers regulate the heart's electrical activity. Other devices include wearable monitors and remote cardiac monitoring systems.</p></p>
