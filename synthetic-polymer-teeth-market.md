<p><p>The Synthetic Polymer Teeth market includes various types, such as PMMA Resin and Acrylic. PMMA Resin teeth are made from a type of synthetic resin called Polymethyl Methacrylate, which is known for its high strength and durability. These teeth are widely used in dental prosthetics due to their natural appearance and resistance to staining. On the other hand, Acrylic teeth are made from a type of thermoplastic polymer known as Polyacrylic, offering good esthetics and affordability. Both types serve as effective alternatives to natural teeth in dental applications.</p></p>
