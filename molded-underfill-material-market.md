<p><p>Molded underfill material is widely used in electronic devices to enhance their reliability and performance. The market for molded underfill materials can be divided into two types: Dynamic Mechanic Analyzer (DMA) Technology and Thermal Mechanical Analyzer (TMA) Technology. DMA technology focuses on analyzing the viscoelastic properties of molded underfill materials under dynamic conditions. TMA technology, on the other hand, examines the changes in these materials' dimensions and stiffness due to temperature variations. Both technologies enable manufacturers to select the most suitable molded underfill materials for their applications, ensuring optimal device performance and durability.</p></p>
