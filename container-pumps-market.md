<p><p>Container pumps are devices used to transfer liquids from one container to another. Canister pumps are compact and portable pumps that are commonly used for transferring liquids from large containers. Siphon pumps rely on gravity and air pressure to transfer liquids and are often used for small-scale applications. Hand pumps are manually operated pumps that require human effort to transfer liquids. Foot pumps, on the other hand, are operated by foot pressure and are commonly used in industrial settings for efficient liquid transfer.</p></p>
