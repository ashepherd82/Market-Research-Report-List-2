<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Meteorological Method Silica</li><li>Gel Method Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silica Extinction Powder market consists of three main types: Precipitated Silica, Meteorological Method Silica, and Gel Method Silica. Precipitated Silica is produced through a chemical process, resulting in fine particles with high absorbency and excellent thermal stability. Meteorological Method Silica is generated by burning natural quartz in an oxyhydrogen flame, creating a porous structure suitable for use in meteorology. Gel Method Silica is made by mixing sodium silicate with acid, yielding a gel that can be dried and ground into a fine powder. Each type has distinct characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
