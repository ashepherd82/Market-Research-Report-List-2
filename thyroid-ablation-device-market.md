<p><p>The Thyroid Ablation Device market includes two major types: Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA) and Microwave Ablation (MWA). RFA employs heat generated from a high-frequency alternating current to destroy thyroid tissues, while MWA uses microwaves to heat and ablate the tissues. Both techniques are minimally invasive procedures that aim to treat thyroid conditions such as benign thyroid nodules and thyroid cancers. These devices offer a safe and effective alternative to surgical interventions, allowing for precise and targeted treatment while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
