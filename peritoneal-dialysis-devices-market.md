<p><strong>The Peritoneal Dialysis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD)</li><li>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Devices market includes two main types: Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). CAPD is a type of dialysis that involves manually exchanging dialysis fluid several times a day by the patient, allowing for greater flexibility and independence. APD, on the other hand, uses a machine called a cycler to automatically perform the fluid exchanges during the night while the patient sleeps, offering convenience and ease of use. Both CAPD and APD are effective treatments for individuals with kidney failure.</p></p>
