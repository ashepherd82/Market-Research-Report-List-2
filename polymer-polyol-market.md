<p><p>The polymer polyol market consists of different types, namely saturated aliphatic polyester polyol, unsaturated aliphatic polyester polyol, and lactone polyester polyol. Saturated aliphatic polyester polyol refers to a type of polymer polyol that possesses high stability due to saturation of its chemical bonds. Unsaturated aliphatic polyester polyol, on the other hand, contains unsaturated links in its structure, making it more reactive and prone to chemical reactions. Lastly, lactone polyester polyol refers to a polymer polyol that incorporates lactone monomers, resulting in enhanced flexibility and hydrolytic stability.</p></p>
