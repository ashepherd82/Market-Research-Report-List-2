Thrombin-based sealants work by converting fibrinogen into fibrin, forming a clot. Gelatin-based sealants provide a barrier to prevent leakage after surgery. Other types of sealants include those made from synthetic materials or a combination of natural and synthetic components. These adhesive sealant options aim to improve patient outcomes and reduce complications in surgical procedures.</p></p>
