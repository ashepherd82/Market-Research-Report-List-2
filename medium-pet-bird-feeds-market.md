<p><p>Medium pet bird feeds market consists of two types: staple and supplement. Staple feeds are the primary and essential diet for pet birds, providing them with the necessary nutrients for their overall health and well-being. These feeds usually include a mixture of grains, seeds, and pellets. On the other hand, supplement feeds are additional nutritional options that can be given alongside staple feeds to provide variety and extra nutrients. They may include fruits, vegetables, nuts, or specialized supplements that cater to specific dietary needs or health conditions of pet birds.</p></p>
