<p><p>The personal flotation devices (PFD) market consists of three main types: foam life jackets, inflatable life jackets, and hybrid life jackets. Foam life jackets are made of buoyant materials such as foam and are generally more affordable and durable. Inflatable life jackets are filled with gas upon activation, offering a compact and lightweight design but needing regular maintenance. Hybrid life jackets combine features of both foam and inflatable types, providing a balance of comfort, buoyancy, and ease of use. These various PFD types cater to different preferences and needs in water safety.</p></p>
