<p><strong>The NFC Reader ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The NFC Reader ICs market is classified into four types based on the NFC standards they support. Full NFC Reader ICs are capable of reading and writing data from all NFC-enabled devices. ISO/IEC 15693 reader ICs are specifically designed for contactless smart cards and tags complying with this standard. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A reader ICs support communication with NFC devices operating on this standard. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B reader ICs can communicate with devices operating on both ISO/IEC 14443 Type A and Type B standards. Each market segment caters to specific NFC functionalities.</p></p>
