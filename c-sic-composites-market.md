<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The C-SiC Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) Composites</li><li>Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-SiC composites refer to materials that combine carbon particles or carbon fiber with silicon carbide. Carbon particles and silicon carbide (CP/SiC) composites are created by mixing carbon particles with silicon carbide powder and then consolidating them through a process called liquid silicon infiltration. On the other hand, carbon fiber and silicon carbide (CF/SiC) composites are produced by impregnating carbon fiber mats with silicon infiltration, followed by densification through high-temperature processing. Both types of composites offer high strength, stiffness, and excellent thermal properties, making them suitable for various applications in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
