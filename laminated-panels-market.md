<p><p>The laminated panels market involves the use of various types of laminates, including High Pressure Laminate (HPL) and Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL). HPL is a thick and durable type of laminate that is commonly used in high-traffic areas due to its resistance to scratching, impacts, and heat. On the other hand, CPL is a thinner and more flexible laminate that is often preferred for applications such as furniture and cabinets. Both HPL and CPL offer different advantages and are used in different contexts within the laminated panels market.</p></p>
