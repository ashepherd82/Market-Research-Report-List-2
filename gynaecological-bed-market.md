<p><strong>The Gynaecological Bed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Type</li><li>Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gynaecological bed market offers two main types of beds: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic beds are operated manually, using hydraulic mechanisms to adjust the bed's height, backrest, and leg rest. They are cost-effective and suitable for smaller facilities. Electric beds, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer a wider range of adjustable features, including height, backrest, leg rest, and pelvic tilt. They provide more convenience and flexibility but tend to be more expensive. Both types aim to provide comfortable positions for gynaecological examinations and procedures.</p></p>
