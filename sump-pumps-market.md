<p><p>The sump pumps market offers various types of pumps to cater to different needs. Submersible sump pumps are designed to be installed inside the sump pit and can be fully submerged in water. They are known for their quiet operation and efficient pumping. Pedestal sump pumps, on the other hand, are installed above the sump pit and have a motor mounted on a pedestal. They are easily accessible for maintenance but tend to make more noise. Additionally, there are other types of sump pumps available in the market to serve specific requirements.</p></p>
