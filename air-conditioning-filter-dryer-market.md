<p><strong>The Air Conditioning Filter Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Line Type</li><li>Suction Line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning filter dryer market includes two types: liquid line type and suction line type. The liquid line type filter dryer is installed in the refrigerant line after the condenser to remove any moisture or impurities before reaching the expansion valve. On the other hand, the suction line type filter dryer is placed before the compressor to protect it from any contaminants. Both types play an essential role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of air conditioning systems by ensuring clean and dry refrigerant circulation.</p></p>
