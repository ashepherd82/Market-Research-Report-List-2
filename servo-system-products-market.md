<p><p>The servo system products market comprises two major types: AC servo motors and DC servo motors. AC servo motors are electrical devices that use an alternating current power supply to control the position, velocity, and torque of equipment. They are commonly used in applications where high-speed and high-precision control is required. On the other hand, DC servo motors operate using a direct current power source and are known for their accurate and responsive control performance. These motors are widely used in robotics, automation, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
