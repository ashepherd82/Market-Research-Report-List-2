<p><p>The arterial blood collection devices market includes various types of devices used for collecting arterial blood samples. Blood collection tubes are sterile containers used to collect and store blood samples. Lancets are small, disposable devices used for pricking the skin and collecting blood drops. Needles are used for direct arterial puncture to obtain blood samples. Vacuum blood collection systems use tubes with pre-established vacuum pressure to collect blood. Microfluidic systems use tiny channels and valves to collect and analyze blood samples. Other devices in this market include arterial blood gas samplers and syringes.</p></p>
