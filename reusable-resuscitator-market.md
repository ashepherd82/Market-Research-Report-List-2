<p><strong>The Reusable Resuscitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reusable Resuscitator market consists of two main types: Self-inflating Resuscitators and Flow-inflating Resuscitators. Self-inflating Resuscitators are handheld devices that automatically expand to deliver breaths to a patient when squeezed, providing positive pressure ventilation. These types of resuscitators are commonly used in emergency situations as they are compact and easy to use. On the other hand, Flow-inflating Resuscitators require a continuous flow of oxygen to inflate a bag that delivers breaths to the patient. These resuscitators are mainly used in hospitals for longer-term ventilation requirements.</p></p>
