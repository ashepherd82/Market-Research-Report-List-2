<p><p>The Biaxially Oriented Nylon (BOPA) Film Market consists of two types: Distributed Biaxial Stretching and Simultaneous Biaxial Stretching. In Distributed Biaxial Stretching, the nylon film is stretched in two directions separately, which aids in providing enhanced properties such as tensile strength, transparency, and barrier resistance. On the other hand, Simultaneous Biaxial Stretching involves stretching the film in both directions simultaneously, resulting in lower production costs and improved mechanical properties. Both methods contribute to the diverse applications of BOPA films, including packaging, electrical insulation, and construction.</p></p>
