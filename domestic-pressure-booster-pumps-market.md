<p><p>The Domestic Pressure Booster Pumps market consists of two primary types: Single Stage pumps and Multiple Stage pumps. Single Stage pumps are designed to operate using a single impeller, providing a single level of pressure boost. On the other hand, Multiple Stage pumps utilize multiple impellers to increase the pressure in stages, resulting in higher pressure boost. Both types of pumps are used in domestic settings to enhance water pressure and ensure efficient water supply for various applications such as showers, faucets, and irrigation systems.</p></p>
