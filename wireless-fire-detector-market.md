<p><p>The wireless fire detector market can be categorized into three types: wireless smoke detectors, wireless heat detectors, and wireless multi-sensor detectors. Wireless smoke detectors detect the presence of smoke and trigger an alarm when smoke is detected. Wireless heat detectors monitor changes in temperature and trigger alarms if a rapid increase in heat is detected. Wireless multi-sensor detectors combine both smoke and heat detection capabilities, providing a comprehensive fire detection solution. All these types of detectors communicate wirelessly, allowing for easy installation and monitoring in various settings.</p></p>
