<p><ul><li>EMC Testing</li><li>EMF Testing</li><li>ECU Testing</li><li>EMI Testing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electronics testing market encompasses various types of testing procedures. EMC testing is conducted to evaluate electromagnetic compatibility, ensuring the proper functioning of electronic systems without interference. EMF testing measures electromagnetic fields emitted by electronic components, ensuring they are within safe limits. ECU testing is focused on evaluating electronic control units, which are responsible for controlling various vehicle functions. EMI testing assesses electromagnetic interference to mitigate any potential disruptions caused by electronic systems. Lastly, the "Others" segment includes various additional testing procedures specific to automotive electronics to ensure their reliability and performance.</p></p>
