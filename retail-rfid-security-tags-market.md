<p><p>The Retail RFID Security Tags market encompasses three main types of tags - HF (High-Frequency) Tags, UHF (Ultra-High Frequency) Tags, and LF (Low-Frequency) Tags. HF Tags operate at a relatively short range and are commonly used for items like access control badges. UHF Tags have a longer range and are well-suited for inventory management or supply chain applications. LF Tags have the longest range and are often used for tracking animals or large assets. Each type of tag serves different purposes depending on the required range and application in the retail industry.</p></p>
