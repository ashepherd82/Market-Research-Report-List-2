<p><p>Photo printing refers to the process of producing physical copies of photographs. There are two main types of photo printing markets: film printing and digital printing. Film printing involves developing film negatives and producing prints using traditional darkroom techniques, while digital printing involves printing photographs directly from digital files using inkjet or laser printers. Film printing is usually preferred by professional photographers and enthusiasts who enjoy the traditional process, while digital printing has gained popularity due to its convenience, speed, and the ability to edit photos before printing.</p></p>
