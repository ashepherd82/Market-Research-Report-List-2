<p><p>Textile machinery refers to the equipment used in various processes of textile production. The market is categorized into different types, including spinning machinery, knitting machinery, weaving machinery, finishing machinery, and others. Spinning machinery is used to convert fiber into yarn, while knitting machinery is used to create fabrics from yarn through interlocking loops. Weaving machinery, on the other hand, is utilized to produce woven fabrics by interlacing two sets of yarn. Finishing machinery helps enhance the quality and appearance of textiles. The "others" category includes machinery used for processes like printing, dyeing, and cutting.</p></p>
