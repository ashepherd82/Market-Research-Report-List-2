<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Audio Frequency Transformer market can be divided into three types: Input Transformer, Output Transformer, and Interstage Transformer. </p><p>An Input Transformer is used to match the audio source's impedance to the preamplifier's impedance, ensuring efficient transfer of signals. </p><p>An Output Transformer is responsible for impedance matching and amplification, ensuring optimal signal delivery from the amplifier to the speakers or headphones. </p><p>The Interstage Transformer is placed between amplifier stages to match impedance and transfer signals, enabling efficient transmission and amplification of audio signals. All three transformer types play crucial roles in the audio signal chain to optimize sound quality and performance.</p></p>
