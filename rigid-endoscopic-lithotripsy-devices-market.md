<p><p>Rigid endoscopic lithotripsy devices are used for the fragmentation and removal of stones or calculi within the urinary or biliary systems. Ultrasonic lithotripsy is a type that uses high-frequency sound waves to break down the stones. Ballistic lithotripsy, on the other hand, utilizes laser or electrohydraulic energy to break the stones. Other types of lithotripsy devices may involve pneumatic or mechanical mechanisms. These devices play a crucial role in minimally invasive surgeries, allowing for effective stone removal and reducing the need for more invasive procedures.</p></p>
