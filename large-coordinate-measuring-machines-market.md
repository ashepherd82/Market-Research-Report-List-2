<p><p>The large coordinate measuring machines market encompasses two major types: large gantry coordinate measuring machines and large bridge coordinate measuring machines. Large gantry coordinate measuring machines feature a mobile bridge that moves along a set of rails, enabling measurements of large objects. On the other hand, large bridge coordinate measuring machines have a stationary bridge and a moving table, allowing for measurements of heavy and bulky items. Both types are widely used across industries for accurate and precise measurements of large-scale components.</p></p>
