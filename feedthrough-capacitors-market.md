<p><p>Feedthrough capacitors are electronic components widely used for noise suppression and EMI filtering in various applications. Single layer feedthrough capacitors are designed with a single ceramic layer, providing low inductance and high capacitance. They are suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, multilayer feedthrough capacitors are constructed with multiple ceramic layers, offering enhanced electrical performance and reliability. They can handle high voltage levels and provide excellent attenuation characteristics. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing unwanted electrical disturbances and ensuring proper functioning of electronic devices.</p></p>
