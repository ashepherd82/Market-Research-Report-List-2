<p><p>Pharmaceutical primary packaging label market types refer to different adhesive materials used for labeling pharmaceutical products. Solvent acrylic labels are made using solvents and have excellent resistance to chemicals and temperature. Hotmelt rubber labels are created using heat and pressure and offer good adhesion and flexibility. Water base emulsion acrylic labels are water-based and provide high clarity and moisture resistance. UV curable labels are cured using UV light, ensuring instant drying and high durability. These different types serve varied purposes and cater to specific requirements in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
