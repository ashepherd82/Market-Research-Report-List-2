<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Cutting Machine</li><li>Fiber Laser Cutting Machine</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser cutting machines market can be categorized into three types: CO2 laser cutting machines, fiber laser cutting machines, and others. CO2 laser cutting machines use carbon dioxide gas as the laser source and are suitable for cutting non-metal materials. Fiber laser cutting machines use fiber optics as the laser source and are ideal for cutting various materials, including metals. The "others" category includes laser cutting machines that use different sources or technologies, such as solid-state lasers or crystal lasers, for cutting purposes.</p></p>
