In the market, there are three types of metal sheet bending machines: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. </p><p>Manual metal sheet bending machines require manual labor for feeding, bending, and removing the finished product. They are often compact and suitable for small-scale operations.</p><p>Semi-automatic machines require manual feeding, but the bending process is automated. These machines increase efficiency and accuracy, making them ideal for medium-sized operations.</p><p>Automatic metal sheet bending machines enable fully automated processes with minimal human intervention. They offer high productivity and precision, making them suitable for large-scale industrial operations.</p></p>
