Single-chamber pacemakers have one lead, which is connected to either the right atrium or right ventricle. Dual-chamber pacemakers have two leads, one in the right atrium and one in the right ventricle. Biventricular pacemakers have three leads, allowing for pacing in both ventricles. These different types cater to varying patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
