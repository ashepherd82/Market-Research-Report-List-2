<p><p>Chip beads are electronic components that are commonly used in circuit designs to filter out unwanted signals or noise. There are two main types of chip bead markets: high impedance chip beads and low impedance chip beads.</p><p>High impedance chip beads are designed with higher resistance values, which allow them to provide effective noise suppression and filtering in circuits that require high impedance protection.</p><p>On the other hand, low impedance chip beads have lower resistance values and are primarily used to provide effective filtering in circuits that require low impedance protection, such as power lines or audio applications.</p><p>Both types of chip beads play a crucial role in maintaining signal integrity and reducing interference in electronic circuits.</p></p>
