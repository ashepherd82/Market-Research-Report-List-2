<p><p>A PU (Polyurethane) timing belt is a type of power transmission belt used in various industries to transmit rotational motion between two or more components. PU timing belts are commonly used in applications that require precise positioning and synchronization, such as in automotive engines, robotics, textile machinery, and printing equipment.</p><p>The PU timing belt market has been experiencing steady growth over the years, and this trend is expected to continue during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for automation and robotics in various industries is driving the adoption of PU timing belts for precise motion control. Moreover, the advantages offered by PU timing belts, such as high power transmission efficiency, low noise operation, and resistance to temperature and chemical exposure, are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>In addition, the automotive industry is one of the major end-users of PU timing belts, especially in engine applications.
