<p><p>Audio streaming subscription services offer two main types of subscription options: monthly subscription and annual subscription. </p><p>A monthly subscription allows users to access the service on a month-to-month basis. Subscribers pay a fixed fee each month to stream unlimited audio content. This option offers flexibility for those who prefer a shorter commitment or want to evaluate the service before committing to a longer subscription period.</p><p>On the other hand, an annual subscription requires users to commit to a yearly subscription. Subscribers pay a lump sum for the entire year, usually at a discounted rate compared to the monthly option. This type of subscription is suitable for those who are confident in their long-term usage of the service and are looking for cost savings.</p></p>
