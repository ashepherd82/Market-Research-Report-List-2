<p><p>The cycling refrigerated air dryers market includes two types - air-cooled and water-cooled. Air-cooled dryers use a fan to cool the compressed air, while water-cooled dryers utilize water to remove heat from the compressed air. Air-cooled dryers are popular due to their simplicity and lower cost, as they do not require a water supply. However, water-cooled dryers are more efficient in removing heat and are suitable for applications where ambient temperatures are high or when there is limited space for installation.</p></p>
