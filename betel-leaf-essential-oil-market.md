<p><p>Betel leaf essential oil is produced through two main methods: steam distillation and cold pressing. In steam distillation, the leaves are subjected to steam which helps release the oil, which is then collected. This method is commonly used for producing high-quality and pure essential oils. On the other hand, cold pressing involves mechanically pressing the leaves to extract the oil. This method is mainly used for producing larger quantities of essential oil, but may result in a lower quality product compared to steam distillation.</p></p>
