<p><p>The Medium Pressure UV Curing System market is divided into three types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure refers to curing a small area or specific points, making it suitable for precise applications. Flood Cure is characterized by uniformly curing a larger area, making it ideal for curing entire surfaces. Focused Beam involves directing the UV light onto a specific area with high intensity, enabling rapid curing and efficient energy usage. These market types cater to various needs and applications within the UV curing industry.</p></p>
