<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Oil Debris Monitor market consists of two types: online and offline oil debris monitors. Online oil debris monitors are devices that are installed directly in the oil system and continuously monitor the oil for any debris or particles. They provide real-time data and alerts about the condition of the oil. Offline oil debris monitors, on the other hand, require the oil to be collected and sent to a laboratory for analysis. These monitors are used less frequently but still provide valuable information about the presence and severity of debris in the oil.</p></p>
