<p><p>Ductless solutions refer to heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, which do not require ductwork for operation. There are two types of markets for ductless solutions: Single Zone and Multi Zone. Single Zone refers to systems designed to heat or cool a single room or area, using one outdoor unit and one indoor unit. Multi Zone, on the other hand, is designed to provide heating and cooling in multiple rooms or areas, using multiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit. Both options offer efficient and flexible HVAC solutions for various spaces.</p></p>
