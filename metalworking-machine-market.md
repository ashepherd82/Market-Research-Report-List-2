<p><p>The metalworking machine market can be categorized into two main types: metalforming and metalcutting. Metalforming machines are used to shape metal into different forms such as bending, rolling, and die-casting. These machines play a crucial role in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction. On the other hand, metalcutting machines are used to remove material from metal objects through processes like drilling, milling, and turning. They are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, fabrication, and engineering. Both metalforming and metalcutting machines are essential in the metalworking industry for various applications.</p></p>
