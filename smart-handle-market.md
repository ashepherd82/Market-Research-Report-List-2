<p><p>The Smart Handle market can be categorized into two main types: Active Smart Handle and Passive Smart Handle. </p><p>Active Smart Handles are equipped with sensors and technology that actively interact with the user and the surrounding environment. They may have features like touchscreens, voice recognition, and integrated connectivity, allowing users to control various functions. These handles can offer convenience and enhanced functionality.</p><p>On the other hand, Passive Smart Handles are designed to provide a more seamless and intuitive user experience. They typically rely on technologies like proximity sensors, Bluetooth, or NFC to detect and respond to user actions. These handles may automatically unlock or open when the user approaches, eliminating the need for physical contact or manual operation.</p></p>
