<p><p>Silicone-based antifoam market types can be categorized into oil, solution, emulsion, and solid forms. The oil-based antifoam is manufactured by dispersing silicone oil into a hydrocarbon carrier. Solution antifoams are oil-based antifoams that are diluted in a solvent. Emulsion antifoams consist of silicone oil dispersed in water with the help of an emulsifier, making it suitable for water-based applications. Lastly, solid antifoams are typically in powder or granular form, which can be easily added to various processes to control foam formation.</p></p>
