<p><strong>The Smart Connected HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Split Systems</li><li>Multi Split Systems</li><li>VRF Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Connected HVAC market encompasses three main types of systems: Single Split Systems, Multi Split Systems, and VRF Systems. Single Split Systems consist of one indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit, ideal for cooling individual rooms. Multi Split Systems can connect multiple indoor units to one outdoor unit, enhancing flexibility and cooling larger areas. VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) Systems enable different indoor units to operate at varying temperatures simultaneously, providing energy-efficient and customizable cooling solutions for larger spaces. All three types offer smart connectivity features, allowing remote control and monitoring through mobile devices.</p></p>
