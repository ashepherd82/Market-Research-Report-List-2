<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Pressure Vessel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PAN-Based</li><li>Pitch-Based</li><li>Viscose-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber pressure vessel market consists of three major types: PAN-based, pitch-based, and viscose-based. PAN-based glass fiber pressure vessels are created using polyacrylonitrile fibers and offer excellent strength and rigidity. Pitch-based vessels are made from petroleum or coal tar pitch and are known for their high resistance to corrosion and thermal stress. Viscose-based vessels are manufactured from viscose rayon fibers and provide good resistance to chemicals. Each type has distinct characteristics and applications, catering to different industry requirements in terms of strength, resistance, and durability.</p></p>
