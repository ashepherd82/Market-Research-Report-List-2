<p><p>Embedded microprocessors, micro-controllers, and embedded digital signal processors (DSPs) are three types of chips used in the embedded chip market. </p><p>Embedded microprocessors are designed to handle general-purpose computing tasks and have a wide range of applications in various devices. They are powerful and offer a high level of flexibility.</p><p>Micro-controllers, in contrast, are specialized chips that focus on controlling specific tasks within a device. They are commonly used in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial applications.</p><p>Embedded DSPs are specifically designed to process and manipulate digital signals in real-time. They are commonly used in audio and video processing, telecommunications, and medical imaging devices.</p></p>
