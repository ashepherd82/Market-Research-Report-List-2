<p><p>A Fluidized Bed Incinerator is a type of waste management system that utilizes a process known as fluidization to efficiently burn and dispose of waste materials. In this system, solid waste is mixed with a fluidizing medium, typically sand or limestone, which creates a fluidized bed. The waste is then introduced into the bed where it is heated to high temperatures, typically around 850-950 degrees Celsius, causing it to combust and convert into byproducts such as ash and gases.</p><p>The Fluidized Bed Incinerator Market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
