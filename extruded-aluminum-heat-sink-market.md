<p><p>The Extruded Aluminum Heat Sink Market can be categorized into two types: Active Extruded Aluminum Heat Sink and Passive Extruded Aluminum Heat Sink. Active heat sinks utilize additional mechanisms such as fans or blowers to enhance heat dissipation from electronic components. On the other hand, passive heat sinks rely solely on natural convection for heat transfer. Both types aim to efficiently dissipate the generated heat and maintain the optimum temperature of electronic devices, but active heat sinks offer higher cooling capabilities due to the aid of external cooling mechanisms.</p></p>
