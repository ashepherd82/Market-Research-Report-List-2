The increasing adoption of electronic health records (EHRs) and the growing demand for efficient data storage and management solutions in the healthcare sector are the primary drivers of market growth. One of the key trends is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into cloud computing platforms to enhance data analytics capabilities. This enables healthcare providers to gain valuable insights from large volumes of data, leading to improved patient care and outcomes.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of hybrid cloud solutions in healthcare organizations. Hybrid cloud combines both private and public cloud environments, offering greater control over sensitive data while leveraging the benefits of public cloud infrastructure.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of cloud computing in healthcare.
