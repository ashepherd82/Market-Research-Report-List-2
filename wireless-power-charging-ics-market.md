Wireless Power Receiver ICs are integrated circuits designed to receive power wirelessly from a transmitter and convert it into usable energy for charging devices. On the other hand, Wireless Power Transmitter ICs are integrated circuits that generate a wireless power signal and transmit it to the receiver ICs. Both types of ICs play a crucial role in enabling wireless charging technology for various electronic devices.</p></p>
