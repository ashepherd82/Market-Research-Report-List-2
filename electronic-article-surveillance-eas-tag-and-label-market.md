<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Tag and Label market includes various types such as hard, soft, and permanent deactivation. Hard EAS tags are physically attached to items and can only be removed with a specialized tool. Soft EAS tags, on the other hand, are easily removed and can be reattached to different products. Permanent deactivation EAS labels are typically applied during manufacturing and cannot be reactivated once deactivated at the point-of-sale. These different types of tags and labels offer retailers flexibility in implementing theft prevention measures based on their specific needs.</p></p>
