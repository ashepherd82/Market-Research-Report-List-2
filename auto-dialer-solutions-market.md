<p><p>Auto dialer solutions are software tools used in call centers to automatically dial a large number of phone numbers. The market for auto dialer solutions can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based auto dialer solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-premises auto dialer solutions, on the other hand, are installed directly on the organization's hardware, providing more control and security. Both types cater to the needs of call centers, but differ in terms of deployment and operational characteristics.</p></p>
