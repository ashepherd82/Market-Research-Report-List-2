<p><p>Microscope objectives are classified into three types based on their magnification power: Max 10x, Max 50x, and Above 50x. The Max 10x objectives have a maximum magnification of 10 times, providing a wider field of view and are commonly used in educational settings and routine laboratory work. The Max 50x objectives offer a higher magnification for more detailed observations, suitable for advanced scientific research. The Above 50x objectives provide the highest magnification power, enabling researchers to view extremely small details with great precision, crucial for specialized applications like microbiology or nanotechnology research.</p></p>
