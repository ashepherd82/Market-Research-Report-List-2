<p><p>The Smart Infrared Temperature Sensors market consists of two main types: Contact Type and No-Contact Type. Contact Type sensors require physical contact with the object being measured to accurately determine its temperature. On the other hand, No-Contact Type sensors can measure temperature from a distance without any physical contact. This is achieved by detecting and analyzing the infrared radiation emitted by the object. Both types have their own applications and advantages, depending on the specific requirements and circumstances of the temperature measurement task.</p></p>
