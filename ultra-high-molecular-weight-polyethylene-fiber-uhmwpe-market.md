Firstly, the growing awareness and adoption of advanced protective equipment in defense and law enforcement agencies are fueling the demand for UHMWPE fibers for manufacturing bulletproof vests and helmets. Additionally, the increasing use of lightweight materials in the aerospace industry to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions is driving the demand for UHMWPE fibers in aircraft components.</p><p>Moreover, the medical industry is also witnessing a surge in the application of UHMWPE fibers for manufacturing orthopedic implants and surgical sutures due to their excellent biocompatibility and wear resistance.
