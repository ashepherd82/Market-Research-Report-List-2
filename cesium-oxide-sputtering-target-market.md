<p><p>The Cesium Oxide Sputtering Target market comprises two main types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type targets. In the Rotary Type market, the target material is mounted on a rotating holder, allowing for a more uniform erosion of the target during the sputtering process. On the other hand, Non-Rotatable Type targets remain stationary throughout the sputtering process, which may lead to uneven erosion. Both types serve various applications in industries such as electronics, solar cells, and automotive, depending on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
