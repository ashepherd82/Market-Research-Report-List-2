<p><p>AMHS, or Automated Material Handling System, is a key component in the Semiconductor market types such as STK, OHT, OHS, RGV, and AGV. STK (Stocker) refers to a vertical storage unit for wafers, OHT (Overhead Hoist Transport) is an automated vehicle used to transport materials vertically and horizontally, OHS (Overhead Shuttle) is a fast and efficient transport system for wafers, RGV (Rail Guided Vehicle) is a transport system on tracks, and AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle) refers to a vehicle equipped with sensors that can navigate and transport materials autonomously. These systems improve efficiency, productivity, and enable a smooth operation in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
