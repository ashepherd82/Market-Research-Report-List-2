<p><p>The Generator Circuit Breaker (GCB) market refers to the global market for circuit breakers specifically designed for use in power generation applications. This market can be categorized into three types: vacuum circuit breakers, SF6 circuit breakers, and others. Vacuum circuit breakers use a vacuum to extinguish the arc, while SF6 circuit breakers utilize sulfur hexafluoride gas for arc extinction. The "others" category includes various types such as air circuit breakers, oil circuit breakers, and hybrid circuit breakers. These GCBs are essential in ensuring the safe and reliable operation of power generation systems.</p></p>
