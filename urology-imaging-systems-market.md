<p><p>The Urology Imaging Systems market includes various types of imaging systems used in the field of urology. Conventional Radiology Urology Imaging Systems refer to X-ray machines and film-based imaging systems commonly used for basic diagnostic purposes. Ultrasound Urology Imaging Systems use sound waves to create images of urinary organs and are non-invasive. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of the urinary tract. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-rays and computer processing to produce cross-sectional images of the urinary system.</p></p>
