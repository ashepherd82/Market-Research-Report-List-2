<p><p>The market for Confocal Fiber Displacement Sensors is divided into two types: Straight Type and Right-Angle Type. The Straight Type sensor is designed to measure distances in a straight line, making it suitable for applications where the target and the sensor can be positioned in a direct line. On the other hand, the Right-Angle Type sensor is designed to measure distances at right angles, making it ideal for applications where the target and the sensor need to be positioned perpendicular to each other. These two types cater to different measurement scenarios in various industries.</p></p>
