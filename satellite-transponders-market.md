<p><p>Satellite transponders are used to receive signals from Earth stations, amplify them, and retransmit them back to the ground. They operate in different frequency bands, namely, C-band, KU-band, and KA-band. C-band operates at lower frequencies and provides wider coverage but has limited bandwidth. KU-band operates at higher frequencies, offering more bandwidth and higher data rates, but with a narrower coverage area. KA-band operates at even higher frequencies and provides the highest data rates but with limited coverage, making it suitable for high-demand applications like broadband connectivity.</p></p>
