<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive/Self-Adhesive Labelers</li><li>Stretch Sleeve/Shrink-Sleeve Labelers</li><li>Glue-Based Labelers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated labeling machine market is divided into various types, including pressure sensitive/self-adhesive labelers, stretch sleeve/shrink-sleeve labelers, and glue-based labelers. Pressure sensitive/self-adhesive labelers apply labels with an adhesive that sticks directly to the product. Stretch sleeve/shrink-sleeve labelers use heat to shrink a plastic film onto the product, creating a tight-fitting label. Glue-based labelers apply labels using an adhesive substance. These machines cater to different labeling needs, providing efficient and accurate labeling solutions for various industries and products.</p></p>
