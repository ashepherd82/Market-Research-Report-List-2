<p><p>The laparoscopic hand instrument market consists of two main types: reusable hand instruments and disposable hand instruments. Reusable hand instruments are designed to be sterilized and used multiple times, making them a cost-effective option in the long run. On the other hand, disposable hand instruments are intended for single-use and are discarded after each procedure. This eliminates the need for sterilization and reduces the risk of cross-contamination.
