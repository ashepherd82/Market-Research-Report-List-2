Veterinary oximeters can be handheld or tabletop devices, and they are utilized in various veterinary settings including hospitals, clinics, and research laboratories.</p><p>The veterinary oximeter market is witnessing significant growth due to the growing pet adoption rates and increasing awareness about animal healthcare.
