<p><p>The self-cleaning candle filter market is categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. In the vertical market, the filters are designed to be installed in an upright position, allowing the filtering process to occur from top to bottom. This arrangement is commonly used for applications where gravity can assist in the filtration process. On the other hand, the horizontal market involves filters that are installed in a horizontal position. This arrangement is often preferred for applications with limited vertical space, as it allows for efficient filtration in a compact design.</p></p>
