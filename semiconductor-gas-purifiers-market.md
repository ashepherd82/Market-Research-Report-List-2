<p><ul><li>Point of Use Gas Purifiers</li><li>Bulk Gas Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor gas purifiers are specialized devices used in the semiconductor industry to remove impurities from gases used in manufacturing processes. There are two main types of semiconductor gas purifiers: point of use gas purifiers and bulk gas purifiers. Point of use gas purifiers, as the name suggests, are small devices installed at the point where gases are used in the manufacturing process. They purify the gases on-site, ensuring high-quality gas delivery. On the other hand, bulk gas purifiers are larger systems that purify gases in large volumes, typically at centralized locations before they are distributed to multiple points of use.</p></p>

Etching processes use purifiers to eliminate contaminants from the etchant gases.
