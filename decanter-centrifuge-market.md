<p><p>The Decanter Centrifuge Market consists of two main types: three-phase Decanter Centrifuge and two-phase Decanter Centrifuge. A three-phase decanter centrifuge is designed to separate a mixture into three components - solids, liquid, and liquid phase waste. It is commonly used in industries such as chemical, food, and wastewater treatment. On the other hand, a two-phase decanter centrifuge only separates the mixture into two components - liquid and solids. These centrifuges find applications in various industries like oil and gas, mining, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
