<p><p>Fish feed dryers are used to remove moisture from fish feed to enhance its shelf life and prevent spoilage. There are two types of fish feed dryers available in the market: sunken type and floating type. Sunken type fish feed dryers consist of a fixed chamber where heat is generated internally, allowing the feed to sink and dry evenly. On the other hand, floating type dryers consist of a rotating drum that suspends the fish feed in hot air, ensuring uniform drying. Both types offer effective and efficient drying solutions for fish feed producers.</p></p>
