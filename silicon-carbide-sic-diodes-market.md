<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) Diodes are electronic devices that utilize the properties of silicon carbide material for efficient power conversion applications. Single diodes are designed to handle lower power levels and are commonly used in consumer electronics and low voltage applications. Dual diodes, on the other hand, consist of two diodes integrated into a single package and are suitable for high power applications such as automotive electronics and industrial power supplies. Both types provide improved efficiency, higher temperature tolerance, and faster switching capability compared to traditional silicon-based diodes.</p></p>
