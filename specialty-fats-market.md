<p><p>Specialty fats market types include Cocoa Butter Equivalent (CBE), Cocoa Butter Substitute (CBS), Cocoa Butter Replacer (CBR), Milk Fat Replacer (MFR), and Cocoa Butter Improver (CBI). These fats find applications in various products such as margarine, shortening, frying oil, and spraying oil. CBE is used as a substitute for cocoa butter, CBS and CBR mimic the properties of cocoa butter, while MFR provides an alternative to milk fat. CBI enhances the texture and taste of cocoa butter-based products. Margarine, shortening, frying oil, and spraying oil are different specialty fat-based products commonly used in cooking and food processing.</p></p>
