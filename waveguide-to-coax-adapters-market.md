<p><p>Waveguide to Coax Adapters are used to connect waveguide transmission lines to coaxial cables. They come in two market types: Female / Jack and Male / Plug. "Female / Jack" refers to the adapter with a female connector on one end that can accept a waveguide male connector, while the other end has a coaxial female connector to connect to a coaxial cable.
