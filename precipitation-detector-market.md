<p><strong>The Precipitation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weighing</li><li>Tipping Bucket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precipitation detector market consists of different types of detectors, including Weighing and Tipping Bucket detectors. Weighing detectors accurately measure the amount of precipitation by continuously monitoring the weight of the collected precipitation. On the other hand, Tipping Bucket detectors count the number of times a bucket tips, which helps estimate the volume of precipitation. Both types of detectors are commonly used in meteorology and climate research to gather data on various types of precipitation, such as rainfall and snowfall.</p></p>
