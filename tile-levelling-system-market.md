<p><strong>The Tile Levelling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wedge System</li><li>Screw Cap System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile leveling system market consists of two main types: the wedge system and the screw cap system. The wedge system utilizes plastic wedges to create a level surface between tiles, ensuring a consistent and even appearance. On the other hand, the screw cap system employs caps and threaded posts to adjust the height of tiles, allowing for precise leveling and alignment. Both systems serve the purpose of simplifying the tile installation process, reducing tile lippage, and achieving a professional finish.</p></p>
