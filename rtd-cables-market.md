<p><strong>The RTD Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Wire</li><li>3 Wire</li><li>4 Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) cables are used for measuring temperatures accurately. The market for these cables is categorized into three types - 2 wire, 3 wire, and 4 wire. In 2 wire RTD cables, the sensor and connections are formed by two wires, which can cause errors due to lead resistance. 3 wire RTD cables use an additional wire to compensate for the lead resistance, minimizing measurement inaccuracies. 4 wire RTD cables provide the most accurate temperature measurements as they separate the sensor and the measuring circuit, eliminating lead resistance.</p></p>
