<p><p>Polyimide flexible heaters are thin, lightweight heating solutions that provide efficient and consistent heat transfer in various industries. These heaters are made from a polyimide film, which is a polymer material known for its exceptional thermal stability, electrical insulation properties, and resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. The market growth can be attributed to various factors, including the increasing demand for advanced heating solutions in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.
