<p><p>A line thrower is a device used for rescue and maritime operations to throw a line from one point to another. There are two main market types for line throwers: pneumatic throwers and gunpowder projectors. Pneumatic throwers use compressed air or gas to propel the line, providing a safer and more controlled operation. On the other hand, gunpowder projectors rely on the explosive force of gunpowder to launch the line, offering a more powerful and long-range solution. Both market types serve the same purpose but differ in their mechanisms and characteristics.</p></p>
