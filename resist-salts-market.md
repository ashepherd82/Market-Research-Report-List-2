<p><p>Resist salts are used in the dyeing process to create patterns or designs on fabrics. The market types of resist salts include crystalline powdered resist salt, granular resist salt, and others. Crystalline powdered resist salt refers to finely ground salts that are easily dissolved in water, providing a smooth application on fabrics. Granular resist salt, on the other hand, consists of larger particles that are mainly used for tie-dye techniques or creating textured effects. Lastly, the "others" category includes various forms of resist salts such as liquid or gel-like solutions. These different market types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of fabric dyers.</p></p>
