<p><p>The chromatography vial market is divided into three main types: liquid chromatography (LC), gas chromatography (GC), and others. LC vials are used for separating mixtures in liquid phase, utilizing a stationary phase and a mobile phase. GC vials are specifically designed for gas-phase separations, using a gas as the mobile phase. The "others" category includes vials used in specialized chromatography techniques such as supercritical fluid chromatography (SFC) or thin-layer chromatography (TLC), which have their unique separation methods and vial requirements.</p></p>
