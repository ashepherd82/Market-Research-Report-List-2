<p><p>The market for Integrated Power Distribution Units (PDUs) can be categorized into three types: single-phase, three-phase, and others. </p><p>Single-phase PDUs are designed to distribute electrical power in residential and small commercial settings. They deliver power through a single alternating current (AC) waveform, commonly used for household appliances, lighting, and low-power computers.</p><p>Three-phase PDUs are mainly utilized in industrial and large commercial applications. They distribute power through three alternating current waveforms, providing higher power capacity and stability required for heavy machinery and large-scale operations.</p><p>The "others" category comprises specialized PDUs catering to specific needs such as rack-mount PDUs, floor-mounted PDUs, and modular PDUs. These PDUs offer customized power distribution solutions for different environments and equipment configurations.</p></p>
