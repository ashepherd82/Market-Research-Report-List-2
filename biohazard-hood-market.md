<p><p>The Biohazard Hood market includes different types: Type A and Type B. Type A hoods are used to provide protection against biological agents and toxins commonly found in microbiology labs, hospitals, and pharmaceutical facilities. They maintain negative pressure, preventing contaminated air from escaping the hood. Type B hoods, on the other hand, offer additional protection against chemical fumes and vapors. Suitable for working with hazardous chemicals, they are equipped with activated carbon filters to capture and neutralize airborne chemicals. Both types ensure a safe working environment for researchers and technicians dealing with hazardous materials.</p></p>
