<p><p>Clinical diagnostics refers to the process of analyzing patient samples to diagnose, monitor, or screen for diseases, infections, or other medical conditions. The market growth is driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic and infectious diseases, advancements in diagnostic technologies, and rising demand for personalized medicine.</p><p>One major trend influencing the clinical diagnostics market is the shift towards point-of-care testing (POCT). POCT offers several advantages such as fast results, immediate decision-making, and convenience for patients, leading to its widespread adoption. Moreover, the growing focus on early disease detection and prevention is driving the demand for molecular diagnostics, which enable rapid and accurate diagnosis. Additionally, the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning in diagnostics is improving the accuracy and efficiency of testing processes.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on the clinical diagnostics market.
