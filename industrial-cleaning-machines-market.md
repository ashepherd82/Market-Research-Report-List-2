<p><p>The industrial cleaning machines market comprises three main types: ride-on industrial cleaning machines, walk-behind industrial cleaning machines, and automatic industrial cleaning machines. Ride-on machines are specifically designed for larger spaces and are operated by an individual who rides on the machine during the cleaning process. Walk-behind machines are more suitable for smaller areas and are operated by a person walking behind the machine. Automatic machines, as the name suggests, operate autonomously and require minimal human intervention. These different types cater to different cleaning needs and environments within the industrial sector.</p></p>
