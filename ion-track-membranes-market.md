<p><p>Ion track membranes are highly porous, thin films made from a variety of polymer materials such as polyethylene terephthalate (PET) or polycarbonate (PC), which have been chemically etched with ion tracks. These ion tracks are formed by high-energy ion beams, which create cylindrical channels through the thickness of the membrane. These membranes are widely used in various applications such as filtration, gas separation, fuel cells, and biological studies due to their unique properties such as high porosity, small pore size, and excellent mechanical strength.</p><p>The ion track membranes market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
