<p><p>The Allergy Relief Eye Drops market is comprised of two main types: Seasonal Allergic Conjunctivitis (SAC) and Perennial Allergic Conjunctivitis (PAC). SAC refers to allergies that occur during specific seasons, such as spring or fall, due to outdoor allergens like pollen. PAC, on the other hand, refers to year-round allergies caused by indoor allergens like dust mites or pet dander. Both types require eye drops for relief, with SAC being more common during specific seasons and PAC being a continual issue throughout the year.</p></p>
