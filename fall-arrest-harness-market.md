<p><p>A fall-arrest harness is a piece of personal protective equipment (PPE) designed to prevent fall injuries in work-at-height environments. It typically consists of straps, buckles, and anchor points that secure a person to an anchorage system, effectively stopping them from falling if they lose their balance or footing. Fall-arrest harnesses are widely used in industries such as construction, oil and gas, telecommunications, and utilities, where employees often work at height.</p><p>The fall-arrest harness market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to an increased emphasis on worker safety and stringent regulations regarding fall protection.
