<p><ul><li>Direct-acting Type</li><li>Sub-step Direct-acting Type</li><li>Pilot-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Four-Way Solenoid Valve market comprises three main types: Direct-acting Type, Sub-step Direct-acting Type, and Pilot-type. Direct-acting Type valves have a simple structure where the solenoid directly drives the valve spool. Sub-step Direct-acting Type valves have multiple solenoids that control the opening and closing of the valve in smaller steps, providing precise control. Pilot-type valves have a small pilot valve that controls a larger valve, allowing for high flow rates. These types cater to different applications based on the required level of control and flow rate.</p></p>
