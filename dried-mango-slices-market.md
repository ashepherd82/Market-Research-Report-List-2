<p><p>The market for dried mango slices can be categorized into three types: freeze dried, vacuum drying, and others. Freeze drying involves removing moisture from the mango slices by freezing them and then subjecting them to a vacuum, resulting in a final product with a light and crispy texture. Vacuum drying involves removing moisture from the mango slices by placing them in a vacuum chamber and applying low heat, resulting in a chewy and preserved texture. The "others" category includes other drying methods such as sun drying or dehydration using hot air or microwave.</p></p>
