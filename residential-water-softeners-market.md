<p><ul><li>Salt Based Water Softeners</li><li>Salt Free Water Softeners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water softeners market consists of two main types: salt-based water softeners and salt-free water softeners. Salt-based water softeners use a process called ion exchange, where sodium ions are exchanged with hardness minerals like calcium and magnesium, resulting in softened water. On the other hand, salt-free water softeners use alternative technologies like template-assisted crystallization or magnetic fields to prevent the minerals from adhering to surfaces and causing scale buildup, achieving a similar effect as salt-based softeners without the need for adding sodium to the water.</p></p>
