<p><p>The EVA-based solar encapsulants market includes two major types: EVA Film and EVA Sheet. EVA Film is a thin layer of ethylene-vinyl acetate that is used to encapsulate the solar cells in solar panels. It provides protection against moisture, UV radiation, and mechanical stress. On the other hand, EVA Sheet is a thicker form of EVA material that offers enhanced insulation and electrical properties. Both EVA Film and EVA Sheet play crucial roles in ensuring the durability, efficiency, and longevity of solar panels.</p></p>
