<p><p>Silicone diffusion pump fluids are specialized oils used in high vacuum systems to maintain a vacuum environment. The market includes two types: low viscosity silicone oil and high viscosity silicone oil. Low viscosity silicone oil has a thinner consistency, allowing for faster vapor flow and higher ultimate vacuum levels. It is typically used in applications requiring rapid pump-down times. On the other hand, high viscosity silicone oil is thicker, providing better lubrication properties and resistance to thermal degradation. It is commonly used in high-pressure and high-temperature environments for longer pump life.</p></p>
