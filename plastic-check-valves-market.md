<p><p>Plastic Check Valves in the market can be classified into two types based on their connection method and construction design. The first type is Insert Connection valves, which are designed with a press-fit connection for easy installation into plastic tubing or pipes. These valves feature an internal mechanism that allows fluid flow in one direction and prevents backflow. The second type is Threaded Construction valves, which have threaded ends for connection to threaded fittings or pipes. These valves provide a secure and leak-proof connection, ensuring efficient fluid control in various applications.</p></p>
