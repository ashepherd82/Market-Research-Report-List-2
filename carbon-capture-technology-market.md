<p><strong>The Carbon Capture Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Post-Combustion Carbon Capture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon capture technology is of great importance in curbing greenhouse gas emissions. There are three main types of carbon capture technologies prevalent in the market. Pre-combustion carbon capture involves capturing carbon dioxide (CO2) before the fuel is burned. Oxy-combustion carbon capture captures CO2 by burning fuel in pure oxygen rather than air. Post-combustion carbon capture captures CO2 from flue gases after fuel combustion. These technologies aim to reduce CO2 emissions from different stages of the energy production process, fostering a cleaner and more sustainable future.</p></p>
