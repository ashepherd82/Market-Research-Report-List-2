<p><p>Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs) are a class of medications that are commonly used to reduce the production of gastric acid in the stomach. A number of factors contribute to this growth, including the increasing prevalence of gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), growing geriatric population, and the rise in chronic acid-related disorders.
