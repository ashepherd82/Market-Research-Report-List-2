The rising adoption of portable electronics, including smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices, is boosting the demand for conductive rubber in this sector.</p><p>The automotive industry is another significant contributor to the conductive rubber market. Conductive rubber is used in applications such as gaskets, seals, O-rings, and connectors, where it provides electrical conductivity and resistance to harsh environmental conditions.
