<p><strong>The Portable Formaldehyde Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffusion Type</li><li>Pump Suction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Formaldehyde Tester market consists of two main types: Diffusion Type and Pump Suction Type. The Diffusion Type utilizes passive sampling and measures formaldehyde levels by relying on the natural movement of air. This type is suitable for small spaces and offers convenience in terms of usage and portability. On the other hand, the Pump Suction Type actively draws samples into the device for analysis. This type is more effective in larger areas and provides faster and more accurate results due to its active sampling method.</p></p>
