<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Jig saw blades are categorized into two types based on the design of the blade shank: T-shank and U-shank. T-shank blades have a straight shank with a T-shaped end that fits into a corresponding T-shaped slot in the jig saw. These blades are widely used and easily interchangeable across various brands and models of jig saws. On the other hand, U-shank blades have a U-shaped shank that fits into a U-shaped slot in the jig saw. Though less common, U-shank blades offer excellent stability and are popular among professionals for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
