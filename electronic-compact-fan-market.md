<p><p>The electronic compact fan market encompasses various types of fans, including centrifugal fans, axial fans, mixed flow fans, and cross flow fans. Centrifugal fans are designed to create high pressure by directing air radially outwards, commonly used in HVAC systems. Axial fans provide high airflow at low pressure, often used in cooling electronic devices. Mixed flow fans combine elements of centrifugal and axial fans, offering both high airflow and pressure. Cross flow fans move air inward or outward in a perpendicular direction, typically utilized in small electronic devices.</p></p>
