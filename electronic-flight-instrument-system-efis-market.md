<p><p>The Electronic Flight Instrument System (EFIS) is an integrated display system that provides a real-time display of flight information to the pilot. It includes primary flight instruments such as attitude indicators, airspeed indicators, altimeters, and navigational data such as navigation aids and GPS information. EFIS replaces the traditional analog gauges and provides a more concise, accurate, and user-friendly representation of flight information.</p><p>The EFIS market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for aircraft modernization and the adoption of advanced avionics systems. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the major trends in the EFIS market is the integration of enhanced vision systems (EVS) and synthetic vision systems (SVS) into the EFIS display. EVS and SVS technologies provide pilots with improved situational awareness, especially in low-visibility conditions. This integration enhances flight safety and reduces the reliance on external sources of information.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of glass cockpits, which extensively use EFIS displays. Glass cockpits provide a seamless integration of various flight instruments onto a single display, reducing pilot workload and enhancing operational efficiency.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing demand for fuel-efficient aircraft is driving the adoption of lightweight EFIS systems, which reduce the overall weight of the aircraft, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and reduced carbon emissions.</p><p>In conclusion, the EFIS market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the need for aircraft modernization, enhanced safety features, and fuel efficiency. The integration of EVS and SVS technologies, the adoption of glass cockpits, and the development of lightweight EFIS systems are some of the key trends shaping the market.</p></p>
