<p><p>Modular furniture refers to a kind of furniture that can be customized and reconfigured based on the user's needs and preferences. This furniture is typically packaged in a compact box, reducing transportation costs and making it easier to handle. Flat pack furniture has gained popularity due to its affordability, easy transportation, and ease of assembly.</p><p>The modular and flat pack furniture market is experiencing rapid growth, primarily driven by factors such as urbanization, smaller living spaces, changing lifestyles, and increasing disposable incomes.
