<p><p>The portable plasma cutting machine market comprises two main types: manual plasma cutting machines and automatic plasma cutting machines. Manual plasma cutting machines require operators to manually guide the cutting torch along the desired cutting path, providing flexibility and control. On the other hand, automatic plasma cutting machines use computer numerical control (CNC) systems to automatically guide the cutting torch, resulting in precise and efficient cutting without the need for manual intervention. Both types of machines cater to different cutting requirements and offer advantages depending on the specific application.</p></p>
