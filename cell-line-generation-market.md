<p><p>Cell line generation market types refer to various techniques used to create specific types of cell lines for research or drug development purposes. Knockout cell lines involve removing or disabling specific genes in the cell to study their function. Overexpression cell lines involve introducing extra copies of specific genes to study their effects. Inducible expression cell lines allow researchers to control when specific genes are turned on or off. Knockdown cell lines involve reducing the expression of specific genes to study their loss-of-function effects. Knockin cell lines involve introducing specific genetic modifications into the cell. Other types may include tagging or modifying existing genes in cell lines.</p></p>
