<p><p>VHF transmitters are classified into three market types based on their power output: low power, medium power, and high power. Low power VHF transmitters have a lower output power and are typically used for short-range applications such as wireless microphones and small broadcasting systems. Medium power VHF transmitters offer a moderate power output and are commonly used in regional broadcasting or communication systems. High power VHF transmitters provide the highest output power and are utilized for long-range broadcasting, such as TV and radio stations covering large areas. These different market types cater to specific needs and requirements in the field of VHF transmission.</p></p>
