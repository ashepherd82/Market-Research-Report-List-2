<p><strong>The Semiconductor Diffusion Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidation Furnace</li><li>Annealing Furnace</li><li>RTP Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor diffusion equipment market encompasses three main types: Oxidation Furnace, Annealing Furnace, and RTP (Rapid Thermal Processing) Furnace. Oxidation Furnace is used to create oxide layers on the wafer surface. Annealing Furnace is employed to enhance crystal quality and relieve stress in wafer materials. RTP Furnace, on the other hand, allows for quick thermal processing, enabling the formation of thin films and other heat-sensitive processes. These different furnace types cater to various needs in the semiconductor industry, contributing to the overall diffusion equipment market.</p></p>
