<p><p>The consumer electronics electromechanical switch market consists of various types such as tactile, rotary, encoder, toggle, push, detect, micro, dip, and others like power, slide, and joy stick. Tactile switches are used for momentary contact, while rotary switches enable rotation to select electrical contacts. Encoder switches convert rotation into digital signals. Toggle switches are used for on/off operations, while push switches are pressed to complete an electrical circuit. Detect switches activate upon sensing an object. Micro switches are small and versatile, while dip switches provide multiple switch configurations. Other types like power, slide, and joy stick switches cater to specific consumer electronics functionalities.</p></p>
