<p><strong>The Flat-Field Scanning Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>F-theta Scanning Lenses</li><li>Telecentric Scanning Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat-field scanning lens market can be categorized into two main types: F-theta scanning lenses and telecentric scanning lenses. F-theta scanning lenses are widely used in laser scanning systems, providing a flat field of view and uniform magnification throughout the scanning area. On the other hand, telecentric scanning lenses ensure consistent magnification and image quality by offering a parallel optical path and eliminating perspective error. Both lens types are commonly employed in various applications such as 3D printing, laser marking, material processing, and inspection systems.</p></p>
