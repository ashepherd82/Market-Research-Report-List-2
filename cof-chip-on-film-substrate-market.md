<p><p>The COF (Chip On Film) Substrate market includes two types: Single Layer and Double Layer. Single Layer COF substrates have a single layer of film on which the chips are mounted, enabling compact designs and lower costs. On the other hand, Double Layer COF substrates have two layers of film, offering more flexibility in chip placement and greater wiring capabilities. Both types are used in various electronic devices to connect the chips and display panels, providing efficient signal transmission and enhanced performance.</p></p>
