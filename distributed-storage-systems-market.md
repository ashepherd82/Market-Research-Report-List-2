Edge computing requires storage systems that can handle the data processing and storage requirements at the edge of the network, closer to the devices generating the data.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several trends that are expected to shape its growth in the coming years. <p><p>Distributed storage systems refer to the technology that allows data to be stored and accessed across multiple physical devices or locations.
