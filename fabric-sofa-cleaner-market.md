<p><p>The fabric sofa cleaner market can be divided into three types: liquid cleaner, dry cleaning spray, and powder cleaner. Liquid cleaners are typically in the form of a spray or solution that can be directly applied to the sofa fabric. Dry cleaning sprays rely on a solvent-based formula that doesn't require any water and is sprayed onto the surface. Powder cleaners, on the other hand, consist of dry granules that need to be sprinkled over the sofa and then vacuumed off. These different types offer consumers different options based on their preferences and needs.</p></p>
