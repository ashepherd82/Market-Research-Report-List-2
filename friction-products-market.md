<p><p>The friction products market encompasses various types such as brake pads, brake shoes, brake lining, brake rotor, and brake drum. Brake pads are components that apply pressure to the brake rotors to slow down or stop the vehicle. Brake shoes are used in drum brake systems, applying pressure to the inside of a rotating drum. Brake lining is the material used on brake pads and shoes to create friction. Brake rotor is a disc-like component that rotates with the wheel and is clamped by the brake pads. Brake drums are cylindrical-shaped components that work in conjunction with brake shoes to slow down or stop the vehicle.</p></p>
