<p><p>The automotive power discrete market encompasses two main types: GaN (gallium nitride) and SiC (silicon carbide). GaN and SiC are materials used in the manufacturing of power electronics components. GaN offers advantages like improved power density, smaller size, and higher efficiency, making it suitable for applications such as electric vehicles and hybrid vehicles. On the other hand, SiC enables higher voltage handling capabilities and offers better thermal conductivity, making it ideal for applications that require high power and temperature resistance, like in electric vehicles and charging systems.</p></p>
