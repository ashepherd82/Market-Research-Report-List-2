Worm gear reducers have a worm screw and a gear wheel to transmit power at right angles. Planetary reducers consist of several gears arranged around a central gear for enhanced torque capacity. Cycloid reducers utilize cycloidal motion to achieve high efficiency and compact design. Harmonic reducers employ flexible, wave-shaped gears to transmit power smoothly and accurately. Each reducer type offers unique advantages suited for specific applications in intelligent logistics.</p></p>
