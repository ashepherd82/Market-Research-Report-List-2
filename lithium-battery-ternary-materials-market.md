<p><strong>The Lithium Battery Ternary Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Electrode Material</li><li>Negative Electrode Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium battery ternary materials market consists of two main types: positive electrode materials and negative electrode materials. Positive electrode materials are used to enhance the battery's capacity and performance, and are typically made from lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide (NMC) or lithium nickel cobalt aluminum oxide (NCA). Negative electrode materials, on the other hand, are responsible for storing and releasing lithium ions during battery charging and discharging cycles. Commonly used negative electrode materials include graphite and lithium titanate oxide (LTO). These materials play a crucial role in the overall efficiency and durability of lithium batteries.</p></p>
