<p><p>The banjo dulcimer market can be categorized into two types based on the materials used: all solid wood and laminated wood. All solid wood banjo dulcimers are made using solid pieces of wood for the body, neck, and fingerboard, resulting in a rich and resonant tone. These instruments are highly regarded for their superior quality and are often preferred by professional musicians. On the other hand, laminated wood banjo dulcimers utilize thin layers of wood pressed together to form the body and neck. While more affordable, they may not offer the same level of tonal depth and richness as all solid wood models.</p></p>
