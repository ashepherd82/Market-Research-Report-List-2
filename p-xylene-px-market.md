PTA and DMT are key raw materials for the production of polyethylene terephthalate (PET), which is widely used in the packaging industry.</p><p>The P-xylene (PX) market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for PET bottles and containers. PET is extensively used in the beverage and food packaging industry due to its lightweight, durability, and recyclability. This has led to a surge in PX production to meet the growing demand for PET.</p><p>Moreover, the construction and automotive industries have also contributed to the market growth. PX is used in the production of polyester fibers, which find applications in textiles, carpets, and upholstery.
