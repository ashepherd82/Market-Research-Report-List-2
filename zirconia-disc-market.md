<p><ul><li>Standard Translucency (ST)</li><li>High Translucency</li><li>Ultra High Translucency (UHT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zirconia Disc Market Types - Standard Translucency (ST), High Translucency, Ultra High Translucency (UHT) refer to different levels of transparency in zirconia discs used in dentistry. Standard Translucency zirconia offers moderate transparency and is ideal for posterior restorations. High Translucency zirconia provides enhanced translucency and is suitable for anterior and posterior restorations. Ultra High Translucency zirconia offers the highest level of transparency, resembling natural teeth, and is ideal for anterior restorations where aesthetics are critical. These different types allow dentists to choose the most appropriate zirconia disc based on the location and desired appearance of the dental restoration.</p></p>
