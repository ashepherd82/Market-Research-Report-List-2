<p><p>Operational Transconductance Amplifiers (OTA) find extensive applications in various market sectors. In multiplexers, OTAs enable the selection of multiple analog signals to a single output. OTAs are employed in filters to shape and process signals by varying their transconductance. Multiplication operations involving analog signals are facilitated by OTAs in multipliers. Comparators compare varying inputs against a reference voltage, and OTAs play a crucial role in their operation.
