<p><p>Endodontic apex locators are used in dentistry to determine the working length of root canals during root canal treatment. There are two types of apex locators: Electronic apex locators and Traditional apex locators. Electronic apex locators use electronic signals and sophisticated technology to accurately determine the position of the root apex. On the other hand, traditional apex locators rely on electrical resistance measurements to locate the root apex. Both types of apex locators serve the same purpose of aiding dentists in accurately measuring the length of root canals.</p></p>
