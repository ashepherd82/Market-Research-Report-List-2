<p><ul><li>Uncooled Hand Held Thermal Imagers</li><li>Cooled Hand Held Thermal Imagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hand Held Thermal Imagers are devices used for capturing and visualizing thermal images. Uncooled Hand Held Thermal Imagers use microbolometer detectors that operate at room temperature and do not require cooling. They are compact, cost-effective, and suitable for general-purpose applications. On the other hand, Cooled Hand Held Thermal Imagers use cooled detectors based on technologies like Stirling cycle or cryogenic coolers. These imagers offer superior image quality and higher sensitivity, making them ideal for specialized applications such as military and surveillance.</p></p>
