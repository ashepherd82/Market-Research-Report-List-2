<p><strong>The Jet Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apron drive jet bridge</li><li>Nose-loader jet bridge</li><li>Dual jet bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet bridges are a crucial component of airport infrastructure, ensuring smooth and efficient passenger boarding and disembarking from aircraft. There are three main types: Apron drive jet bridges, controlled by a dedicated operator to connect the terminal to the aircraft's front door; Nose-loader jet bridges, designed for nose-loading aircraft, allowing passengers to board directly from the front of the aircraft; and Dual jet bridges, allowing simultaneous boarding from the front and rear doors of larger aircraft, enhancing passenger flow. These market types cater to different aircraft configurations and optimize the boarding process for improved passenger experience.</p></p>
