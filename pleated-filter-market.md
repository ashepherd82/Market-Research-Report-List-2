<p><p>The pleated filter market includes various types of filter cores, such as PTFE, HE, and PP filters. PTFE filter cores are made of a special type of material called polytetrafluoroethylene, which provides excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability. HE filter cores offer high-efficiency filtration, capturing even the smallest particles, making them suitable for critical applications. PP filter cores are made of polypropylene material and are commonly used for general filtration purposes. These different filter core types cater to diverse industrial requirements and provide optimal filtration performance.</p></p>
