<p><p>Capacitive sensors are electronic devices that measure the presence or absence of an object by detecting changes in capacitance. These sensors use electrical fields to detect changes in capacitance when an object enters or leaves the sensor's field. They are commonly used in touchscreens, proximity sensing, level detection, and other industrial applications where accurate and reliable detection of objects is required.</p><p>The capacitive sensors market is experiencing significant growth due to technological advancements and increasing adoption of touch-based devices in various industries.
