<p><p>Clove powder is available in different market types, namely whole grain and crumbs. Whole grain clove powder refers to the powder that is made from grinding whole cloves, including the flower buds, stems, and leaves. This type of powder retains the complete flavor and aroma of cloves. On the other hand, crumbs market refers to the powder that is made from grinding clove crumbs or small pieces, excluding the stems and leaves. This type of powder has a slightly milder flavor but still contains the distinctive taste and scent of cloves.</p></p>
