<p><ul><li>CVD</li><li>HPHT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jewelry synthetic diamonds market consists of two types: CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and HPHT (High-Pressure High-Temperature). CVD diamonds are created by depositing carbon atoms onto a substrate in a controlled environment. This process allows for the growth of high-quality diamonds with precise control over their characteristics. On the other hand, HPHT diamonds are created by subjecting a small diamond seed to high pressure and high temperature, simulating the natural diamond formation process. Both methods produce synthetic diamonds that can be used in jewelry, with CVD diamonds being more widely available in the market.</p></p>
