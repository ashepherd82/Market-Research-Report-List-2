<p><p>A bucket conveyor is a type of conveyor system that uses buckets attached to a rotating chain or belt to transport bulk materials. The continuous bucket conveyor operates consistently, transporting materials without interruption. On the other hand, the pendulum bucket conveyor swings back and forth, creating a pendulum motion. Both types are commonly used in industries such as mining, agriculture, and manufacturing. They efficiently move materials vertically or horizontally, depending on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
