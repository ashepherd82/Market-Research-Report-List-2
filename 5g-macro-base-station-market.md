<p><p>The 5G Macro Base Station market consists of two main types: BBU (Baseband Unit) and AAU (Active Antenna Unit). BBU is responsible for processing and controlling baseband signals, enabling functions like coding, modulation, and decoding. It handles heavy computational tasks. On the other hand, AAU focuses on transmitting and receiving wireless signals, including data and voice. It plays a crucial role in providing strong and reliable wireless coverage. Both BBU and AAU are integral parts of a 5G Macro Base Station, offering essential functionalities for seamless and efficient communication.</p></p>
