<p><p>The cake and patisserie premixes market consists of three types: complete mix, dough-base mix, and dough concentrates. Complete mix is a ready-to-use blend of all the necessary ingredients for making a specific cake or patisserie product. Dough-base mix requires the addition of a few ingredients like water or eggs to create a dough for further processing. Dough concentrates are more concentrated mixes that require the addition of several ingredients, including water, eggs, and fats, to create dough ready for processing. These premixes offer convenience and consistency for professional bakers and foodservice providers.</p></p>
