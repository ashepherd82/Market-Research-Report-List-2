<p><p>The commercial smart glass market encompasses three main types of smart glass: electrochromic glass, thermochromic glass, and photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass can change its transparency based on an electric current, offering control over the amount of light and heat transmission. Thermochromic glass responds to temperature changes by modifying its tint, helping to manage solar heat gain. Photochromic glass features a reversible darkening reaction when exposed to ultraviolet light, providing shading when necessary. These smart glass technologies meet various commercial needs by enhancing energy efficiency, maintaining privacy, and improving occupant comfort in buildings.</p></p>
