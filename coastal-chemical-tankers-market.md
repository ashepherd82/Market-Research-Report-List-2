<p><p>Coastal chemical tankers market is divided into two types: stainless steel and coated. Stainless steel tankers are made from high-quality stainless steel material, ensuring resistance against corrosion and contamination. These tankers are preferred for transporting highly sensitive chemicals and substances that require a high level of purity. Coated tankers, on the other hand, have a protective coating applied on the inner and outer surfaces to prevent reactions with the chemicals being transported. They are commonly used for carrying less sensitive chemicals and are cost-effective compared to stainless steel tankers.</p></p>
