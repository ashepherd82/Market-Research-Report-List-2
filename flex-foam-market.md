<p><strong>The Flex Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flex Foam market includes two main types: Closed Cell and Open Cell. Closed Cell foams are dense and have tightly packed bubbles that are impermeable to air and moisture, making them waterproof and highly insulating. They are commonly used in construction, automotive, and packaging industries. On the other hand, Open Cell foams have interconnected cells that allow air and moisture to penetrate, resulting in a softer and more breathable material. This type is often used in bedding, upholstery, and acoustic applications due to its high comfort and sound absorption properties.</p></p>
