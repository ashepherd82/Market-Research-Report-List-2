<p><p>Rigid food containers are packaging solutions made from materials such as plastic, glass, metal, or paperboard that provide protection and preservation of food products. Firstly, the increasing demand for convenience and ready-to-eat food products has led to the rising need for effective and efficient packaging solutions. Additionally, the growing trends of online food delivery services and take-out meals also contribute to the demand for rigid food containers.</p><p>Another key driver of the market is the increasing concern for food safety and hygiene.
